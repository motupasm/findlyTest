About

Findly is a digital lost-and-found platform designed specifically for universities and colleges, helping students, staff, and campus visitors quickly report, locate, and recover lost items. The platform connects people in real time, making it easy to post details, share images, and track the status of missing belongings — all in one centralized hub.

Return

Found something on campus? Don’t let it sit around or get tossed—help it find its way home with Campus Find! Whether it’s a water bottle, student ID, headphones, or even a backpack, you can easily post a photo and a quick description so the owner can claim it.

It only takes a minute to make someone’s day. Just snap a pic, fill in a few details, and let Findly do the rest. It’s the easiest way to return lost items on campus—and you stay anonymous if you want to.

Found Items

With Findly, you can browse all lost and found reports in one easy-to-use place. No more hunting through random bulletin boards, group chats, or campus offices—everything is organized and searchable, right at your fingertips.

Whether you’ve lost your favorite hoodie, a calculator before your exam, or your entire backpack (it happens), Findly makes it simple to check if someone’s already turned it in. And if you’ve found something lying around, you can quickly see if anyone has reported it missing.
